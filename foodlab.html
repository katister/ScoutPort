Lego Food Lab

How might we help kids understand food?

These images were taken for LEGO Food Lab by me and my co-worker Bruno, a project my team worked on at the Food+Future coLAB. The Lego Food Lab is a concept that leverages the making- and instruction-following components of Lego to teach kids about food. It turns instruction booklets into recipe books, teaching kids about sequencing, flavor, and even culture.
