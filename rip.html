How do you get people to care about Fast Fashion?

We get dressed everyday but are unaware of all the costs to do so. Fast fashion brands like Forever 21, Zara, and H&M are low-priced trendy stores operating at tremendous costs to the earth, the people who make our clothing, and the clothing itself.

Rip Fashion is a brand, campaign, fashion line, and digital store making slow-fashion fashionable.
